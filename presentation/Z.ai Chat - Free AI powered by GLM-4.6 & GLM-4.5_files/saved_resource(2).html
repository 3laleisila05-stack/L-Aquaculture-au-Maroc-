Historical Development of Aquaculture in Morocco

history 1950s: Shellfish Culture Beginnings
• First marine shellfish farms established
• Primarily oyster production (~200 tons)
• Small-scale operations for local markets
waves 1980s: Marine Fish Farming
• Introduction of European seabass and gilthead seabream
• Development of floating cage systems
• Initial export markets to Italy, Spain, France
trending_up 2000s-Present: Modernization & Growth
• Production growth from 1,690 tons (2004) to 3,600 tons
• Expansion of inland aquaculture (carp, trout)
• New regulatory framework and investment initiatives
